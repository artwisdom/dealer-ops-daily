# Welcome to Dealer Ops Daily.

You're now on the list with F&I directors, desk managers, used-car managers, and floor GMs at U.S. dealerships who want a tighter morning briefing than what the trade pubs give them.

Here's what to expect.

---

**What lands in your inbox**

A 5-minute brief, every weekday morning, covering what changed yesterday in:

- **Compliance** — CARS Rule, Safeguards Rule, Holder Rule, state DMV/DOI moves
- **F&I** — product menu economics, attach rates, captive lender rate sheets
- **Used-car desking** — Manheim moves, valuation gaps, retail price velocity
- **Store ops** — DMS/CRM/BDC tech, hiring & comp, fixed-ops margin levers

**What you won't get:**

- 30-paragraph trade-mag features
- OEM-influenced spin
- Anything that requires a paid login to read past the first sentence
- Stock picks, financial advice, or political takes

---

**Why this exists**

The auto trade press is built for executives reading on the way to a manufacturer summit. We're built for the people running the store at 7:30 AM before the first save call.

The brief is **AI-edited and human-reviewed** — we use LLMs to compress 70+ daily sources into the 4–6 stories that matter to a working dealer, then a human editor signs off on every send. (That note appears in every footer too.)

---

**Make it useful in 60 seconds**

Tomorrow you'll get your first real issue. Three things to do now:

1. **Whitelist us:** add `[YOUR_SENDER@dealeropsdaily.com]` to your safe senders. Otherwise day-3 we end up in Promotions.
2. **Reply with one word:** what's the *one* operational beat you most want us to cover? F&I, compliance, used-car, fixed-ops, hiring, tech? Replies tune the source weighting — the more responses, the better the brief gets for your store.
3. **Forward this to one person at the store** who'd find it useful. Day-2 we send a sample issue you can show them; day-3 we'll invite you into the referral program (5 referrals = a quarterly state-of-the-store data pack).

That's it. See you at 6 AM tomorrow.
